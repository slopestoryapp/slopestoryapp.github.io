Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

This privacy policy describes how SlopeStory ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Email address, name, and profile photo when you create an account
  • User Content: Photos, visit logs, and ratings you choose to add
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app to improve our service

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the SlopeStory service
  • To personalize your experience
  • To communicate with you about updates and features
  • To improve our app based on usage patterns

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, with servers located in the United States. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data only with service providers necessary to operate the app (e.g., cloud hosting, analytics).

Your Rights

You can request to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time by contacting us or using the account deletion feature in the app.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at hello@slopestory.com.
